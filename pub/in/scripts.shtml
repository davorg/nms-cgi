This is a list of the programs we have available.

FormMail

nms FormMail is a drop-in replacement for Matt Wright's FormMail script. It converts an HTML form submission to an email message.

Several alternative nms FormMail packages are available. The compat packages are the easiest to install, while the modules packages are targeted at more expert users.
