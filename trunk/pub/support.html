nms support

By filling in your details on this page, you can send a email to the nms support mailing list. This list is a place where you can get help with installing and configuring the nms CGI programs.

Before sending an email to the support list, have you checked if your problem is answered in our FAQ?

When reporting an error to us, simply saying "it doesn't work" is not very much help. Please tell us exactly what errors you see (including any from the web server error log).

It's a good idea to send us the configuration section of the script that you are trying to use. Please don't send us the rest of the script as we already know what that looks like :)

It's also worth checking the support mailing list archives as someone else may have already experienced your problem.

This is almost certainly not the place to be reporting a problem you may be having using a form on someone else's site as the nms support team are not able to fix these. Please contact the owner of the web site directly

If you don't want to use this form, you can also contact the support team by sending an email to nms-cgi-support@lists.sourceforge.net.
