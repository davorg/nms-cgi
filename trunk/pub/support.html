nms support

By filling in your details on this page, you can send a email to the nms support mailing list. This list is a place where you can get help with installing and configuring the nms CGI programs.

Before sending an email to the support list, have you checked if your problem is answered in our FAQ?

This is almost certainly not the place to be reporting a problem you may be having using a form on someone else's site as the nms support team are not able to fix these. Please contact the owner of the web site directly
